LECTURER: MR. NGUYEN MINH LONG

1. Topic 

This project aims to build a synthesized solution for COVID 19 problem – an Automated COVID-free checking system for indoor environment which requires the sophisticated involvement of many types of technologies. The system will include application, AI cameras, sensors whose detailed designed algorithm will be explained in the later parts of this paper. The primary use of this system will be installed for indoor environment that helps us check whether the people are COVID-free without using currently available quick test kit costing more than two hundred VND each time. The system function will have real-time COVID check thus also saving us time. As being able to detect SARS-CoV-2 and its variants, the system helps to reduce the chance of asymptomatic spreading, safeguarding frontline workers and the community and avoiding outbreaks. 

Once the initial trial of this project successes, a higher propose to implement piloting program in a small specific place will be the next step before it could be nationwide adopted. More specifically, RMIT University Vietnam Saigon South Campus could be an ideal environment to test the system as there are technological readiness to support the implementation. The demonstration is shown in Figure 1 that sensor readers will be installed in every entrance point, AI camera will be installed in every critical and high traffic point. Furthermore, inside a building, the senser readers will be installed in every room (Figure 2) so that we can make sure people’s status before granting access for them to the space. By doing this, we are creating a safe environment for every person in such space. 

 

Figure 1: Illustration of sample installation of the system in an environment 

  

Figure 2: Illustration of sample installation of the system inside one building 

 

At the first stage, after successfully piloting at RMIT SGS campus, the collaboration with other organizations to further expand the pilot program could be attempted to test its effectiveness and practicability before introducing the system to the public. Therefore, it could be targeted to reach at least 3000 users from three organizations at the end of piloting. The more users, the higher trustworthiness our system is proved. If this project succeed, further action could be proposed to improve the users experiences as well as incorporating more detecting features. 

In the future, even when the COVID drug treatment is approved, this system is still strongly believed to be beneficial as it helps us to recognize the COVID person more effectively and efficiently. Hence, the action to be taken will be more meaningful to cope with this illness. Furthermore, besides the practical purpose recognizing COVID, the success of such system is supposed to pave the way for further research to the invention of more systems addressing infectious diseases, and any respiratory related diseases.   

 

2. Motivation 

Our motivation 

The SARS-CoV-2 virions (COVID-19 pandemic) – one of the most concerned human’s health crises starting from January 2020 – has hit the world harshly in almost every single field thus leading to a potential great global recession since people all around the world are directed to shut down a large number of community activities. Vietnam was of the top nations performing good COVID-19 prevention policies back in early 2021 that was acknowledged by both many renowned global news and peer-reviewed journals as a success story [2, 3, 4]. Despite being a role model in COVID response strategies, Vietnam still struggles to cope with its fourth wave of the pandemic that many cities tightened the restriction policies and even closed from April 2021 until October 2021 [5]. Eventually, the nation with “0 COVID” policy turned to “living with COVID in a new normal” policy which again proves COVID is inevitable that human must deal with. However, until the drug treatment for COVID is approved, the advent of something could help human to recognized COVID is demanded.  Evidently, the high mortality rate resulting in consecutive disruptions entails severe consequences across the globe [6], [7], [8]. It is fact, Hospitality and tourism industry, for instant, is the most suffering sector since the strict strategies from Vietnamese government of border closures, travel restrictions, quarantine was applied early the pandemic started [9]. Only in this sector, the economic loss hit US$5.9–7.7 billion in only three months [10], which is attributed to 78.7% drop in international tourism [11].  Yet, the current available approaches to prevent the COVIS spread is not strong enough to resume social activities safely due to several reasons. The quick test kit and PCR test, for example, are costly and un-reusable, and not real-time [12], [13]. Firstly, once a guest book the hotel staycation, the person is able to provide the evidence of ‘positive test’ (no infection) by the quick test kit and PCR test within 24 hours. Yet there is no guarantee that this result reflects his/ her true status when the person arrives at the hotel. There is still chance that the person is infected during the travel to hotel, indicating its inability of giving real-time result. Secondly, these tests can only be applicable less than 72 hours, meaning un-reusable after this duration. Thirdly, these tests are costly. As a consequently, the urgent need of a real-time and cost-advantage detecting COVID-19 system is obvious. Therefore, this project acts as a critical contribution to such an uncertainty and volatile situation of COVID that could help us to resume community activities, flattening the curve global recession. 

Why this project is interesting 

Since the limitation of COVID testing products are well perceived by the wide public, if there is someone or an enterprise could introduce a break-through solution to address its constraints they would receive tremendous attention from both academia and practitioners. Indeed, our product will offer such beneficial solution which could help business to build profitable based on the products. Besides, our solution solves the concern of environmental issue compared to the quick test kit since each individual only need to own one sensor and reuse while the quick test kit can only be used once and then through away. Furthermore, if the project is successful, this could also bring many consequences such as threatening the competitors in gadgets detecting COVID-19. Thus, our establishment could change the business landscape greatly. 

How it fits with the current trend 

In recent years, almost everyone owns a smart phone, especially post-pandemic when everything is moving virtually. This phenomenon creates greater demand for virtual connection with their phone. For example, shopping online is more preferable than conventional shopping than before, virtually class is more favored than brick-and-mortar school than before etc. Hence, our product offers a COVID health status on the smartphone in which users can easily share the data within seconds to their organization, to their friends etc. In conclusion, our product is believed to offer the wide communities a great number of advantages. 

What it would show to future employers 

During the planning an implementation phase of the project, the team is able to apply hard skills and technique that we have learnt about software development. Additionally, we also discover and dive into more in-depth step of designing sensors. Although we have less knowledge in mechatronic engineering field, we will seek advice and mentoring from other lecturers and RMIT fellows on this. Therefore, we can learn more from them. This project can prove to future employers that we are willing to learn, we have competence to conduct self-learning, and we are willing to embrace in the project that is challenging and break-through, indicating that we are stepping out of our ‘comfort zone’ to try the new thing without hesitation.

Although the ways our application's data is transmitted and hosted are fairly similar to these other applications, the type of data collected, the method by which collected via the sensor, and the features of the mobile interface would be significantly different from those of those applications. In more detail, all the data (vaccination status, historical data of COVID detecting from sensor, historical data of COVID detecting from AI camera, historical data of entry points, and other popular health indicators such as weight, height, exercise, heart rate, movement, ect.) will be collected and cleaned to visualize on the dashboard of the Application. The users can view the reports, status and comprehensively manage all the system. There are several unique features that other applications are not capable of doing, which makes our product unique selling point.  

First, their data contains only vaccination status, the users must use PCR or quick test kit to check themselves whether they are infected. Afterward, the users must input their status on those application. Thus, these processes are time-consuming, annoyed and they still face the significant dilemma of not real-time data. In other words, these applications are not able to reflect the accurate and legitimate information. In worst case, few ‘evil’ users can input their ‘green status’ while being infected by COVID-19 so as to get access to the place they want. In these scenarios, those applications cannot check whether the information of COVID testing are honest or not. Our application, in contrast, can solve all these critical problems. Indeed, exhaling to the personal tags and passing the places where sensor readers and AI cameras are installed are all the steps our users must do. Antibody detection is carried out via an electrochemical reaction in a hand-held microfluidic device, after which the findings are shown in real-time through our smartphone application. Then the data retrieved from sensor reader and AI camera will be exchanged to the application. In case, the person is green, the door is unlocked for accessing, otherwise, it is closed for entry and the guideline for that user as infected person (F0) is alerted on application. In short, our application is much more convenient and usable rather than just viewing the vaccination status as other applications. 

Second, the current applications available in the market do not allow users to share data digitally, meaning that once users want to show their health status, they must show the phone screen. There are two circumstances in this case:  

  1. Users can show QR code. 

  1. Users access to the specific type of data 

In case (1), the applications default what data to share which dose not allow users to select a single or a couples of data they wish to share. In case (2), the process would be time-consuming since the phone screen would be limited to view more data. This disadvantage also leads to another worth-mentioning problem. When the users want to share specific data but due to phone screen limitation, the viewers want to scroll down. Thus, our users’ phones must be exchanged hands, leading a potential virus transmission. By contrast, Axole application own few features of social media which could help to address these issues. More specifically, Axole allows to add friends and share data with your friends in which you can share as direct message to a specifically selected friends or you can share with your community meaning that anyone who are friends with you on Axole can view. Furthermore, as Axole contain large data of users’ health, the users can select any of them, select time duration of historical data, select to share. In other words, Axole gives users more control over their data sharing, ensuring the data security and privacy level they need.  

Third, Axole is not only the single application, but it is a system containing two other IoT gadgets (sensor and AI camera). Thus, QR codes will also be designated on the personal sensor tag so that we can also have the feature of sharing your data via scanning this QR. This feature is convenient in the case users wish to not take their phone out. 

The three aforementioned beneficial advantages are serving the system users. Besides, governmental institutions and healthcare experts can be benefited from Axole as well. This fundamental application sensor assists in correctly quantifying the body's immunological reaction to a new vaccination, enabling clinicians to make a more accurate diagnosis and treatment decisions. The governmental institutions can use data from this app to effectively monitor the situation thus having the right and appropriate responses to COVID spread.  

Conclusively, the most distinguishing aspect that differentiates us from our others is the availability of devices and a physical system that can communicate with users and provide results immediately. Because many applications do not have this feature, our program will serve as an icebreaker, allowing the users to benefit from the ease, rapidity, and accuracy that our application provides. Additionally, many individuals do not experience any symptoms from COVID 19, but they remain positive; implementing and utilizing our function would result in a safer environment for everybody. We make certain that our application will serve as a life raft for a variety of enterprises, schools, and hospitals. Since medical declarations are not always accurate, our feature should be a more effective technique of assisting in the rapid detection of F0 and the reduction of infections.
