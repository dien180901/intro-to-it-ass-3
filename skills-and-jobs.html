Skill and Jobs

LECTURER: MR. NGUYEN MINH LONG

To advance the business venture, a project team with one background is not enough. For example, if an entrepreneur desires to set up a medical health laboratory, hiring doctors solely cannot contribute to the growth of their business; or if an entrepreneur wishes to set up a restaurant, hiring chefs solely is not enough to run their business. In fact, to run any business model productively and profitably, a collaboration of people who are from diverse backgrounds is indispensable. Any project management cannot miss Team leader position because this vacancy is a critical component of any team's success. The management abilities of the project's leader will determine whether the project will be successful. Thus, the project's leader is also the project's executive director. As a result, if the team lacks a leader, members may encounter disagreements or get disoriented while processing tasks. Hence, it is the leader's responsibility to fix and improve such challenges. In particular, we are looking for a leader who has excellent communication and interpersonal skills (which include the capacity to communicate and deal with individuals individually, in groups, and within teams), problem-solving and decision-making abilities, and the ability to manage work effectively. Furthermore, it is also required to have an overall knowledge and mindset of entrepreneurship who own minimal awareness of finance, marketing, operation in business field, to be in this role.  

Besides the team leader who are the central of the project, if we are given the opportunity to proceed forward with our project, we would want to establish other four positions as follow:  

  • Senior Full-stack developer: this is an indispensable position to our team. Our image will be shown across the website to visitors from all around the globe. Thus, the improvement, creation, maintenance, and development of the website will be the responsibility of the developer. However, since we developed a mobile application, they will also be responsible for the app interface, user interface and user experience (UI/UX), clients, servers, and many other aspects of the app. We will be looking for one who has a fundamental understanding of web languages such as HTML and CSS, problem-solving abilities, technical competence in artificial intelligence, a basic Frontend/Backend foundation, and teamwork experience. 

  • Co-Web Developer/App Developer: It may seem like a new title for a full-stack developer, but it is not. Co-Web Developer/App Developer, this role will monitor the progress of a full-stack developer via the use of an app and the web. They will assist and support the full-stack developer with various tasks, while the fullstack developer is responsible for the app's and the website's main functionality. We aim for someone who meets the same requirements as a full-stack developer, and the most important ability we emphasize is time management. 

  • Data scientist: Because our project will get a large amount of data from a variety of sources, a data scientist will be responsible for maintaining and protecting the data. In addition, they would be regarded as a report writer for our group. For the data scientist role, we require data analyst abilities, academic writing skills, problem-solving skills, and teamwork experience. 

  • Robotics and mechatronics engineer: It took us a long time to integrate and decide our friends' ideas from the area of software engineering because of our past experience that we did not have any robotics or mechatronics training or background. As a result, at this moment, we are actively seeking a robotic/mechatronic engineer who will be able to assist us with testing, technical details, and hardware functionalities. Additionally, we will qualify based on the following factors: the ability to plan and think ahead, the mystery of knowing your robot's body or the mechanical part: (how it is made, what materials it is made of, its strengths, its weaknesses), knowledge of electronic systems, programming language experience (such as Python, C, Java), and mathematical skills. Last but most importantly, this person will act as an expert in developing the sensor who is in charge of leading this sub-team due to their knowledge and expertise in building electrical equipment.
